Objectives of the course for the student
To recognize the significance of using engineering methodologies in software design and development.
To learn how to apply software engineering techniques and methodologies to the development task.
How to develop and construct a software specification.
How to develop and construct a software design.
How to verify and validate software.
To apply various techniques of software management.
To work as the member of a design and development team to develop a product for a real world entity in the community.
To establish connections between academic knowledge and civic life.
To develop professional communication skills by working within and across communities, context, and social structures.
